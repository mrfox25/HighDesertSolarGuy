Sound Familiar?

The High Desert is full of orphaned solar.

During the boom, dozens of installers came through Phelan, Victorville and Hesperia selling panels — then folded. When something breaks, there's no one to call. The warranty paperwork leads to a disconnected number. Sound about right?

📉

Your bill crept back up

The panels are on the roof but the savings disappeared. Often it's a tripped or failed inverter quietly producing nothing.

The app went dark

Monitoring shows offline, "no data," or won't log in. Sometimes the system still works — sometimes it doesn't. You can't tell, and neither can the dead company.

What I Fix

Diagnosis first. Honest answers always.

I start every job the same way: figure out what's actually wrong before anyone spends a dollar on parts. Then the right licensed tradesperson does the hands-on work.

01 · DIAGNOSTICS

System diagnostic & health check

On-site inspection of panels, wiring, and inverter. I tell you in plain English what's working, what isn't, and what it'll take to fix — no upsell.

02 · INVERTER

Inverter repair & replacement

The #1 failure point, especially in High Desert heat. I coordinate licensed C-10 electricians for any inverter or grid-tied work that requires it.

03 · MONITORING

Monitoring reconnection

Get your production data back. I re-establish monitoring on Enphase, SolarEdge, Tesla and other platforms so you can actually see what your system is doing.

04 · WARRANTY

Warranty & manufacturer claims

Even if your installer is gone, the panel and inverter manufacturers' warranties often aren't. I help you track them down and file the claim.
